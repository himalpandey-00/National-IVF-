In Vitro Fertilization(IVF)

Combining eggs and sperm outside the body
in a laboratory and then transfering the
resulting embryo(s) into the uterus.

Intrauterine Insemination(IUI)

Fertility treatment where washed and prepared
sperm is directly placed into the uterus during a
woman's ovulation period, increasing the chances
of fertilization.

Egg Freezing

Harvested woman's eggs, frozen, and
stored for the future use.
